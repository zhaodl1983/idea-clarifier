# Writing Standards

Use these standards for final PRD and gate outputs.

- Use active voice.
- Keep paragraphs short.
- Use numbered lists for ordered steps.
- Use bullets for parallel facts.
- Avoid ambiguous words such as "可能", "后续考虑", "适当", "尽量", and "相关".
- Put uncertainty into `[Assumptions to Validate]`, `[Risks]`, or `[Future Iterations]`.
- Use one term for one concept throughout the document.
- Make every requirement testable.
- Separate goals from non-goals.
- Separate facts from assumptions.
